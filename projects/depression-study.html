Student Mental Health Analysis

Explored 27,901 student surveys to uncover structural patterns of depression across academic, lifestyle, and psychological factors.

Excel Tableau Data Analysis

Project Overview

Analyzed survey data using Excel PivotTables to structure key metrics and compare groups. Built a Tableau dashboard that makes complex data easy to explore and understand for decision-makers. Delivered clear insights that can support universities or organizations in identifying student risk patterns.

Role

Data Analyst

Skills

Data analysis, data cleaning, cohort comparison, Tableau, Excel

Key Insights

Gender Disparity

Significant differences in depression prevalence observed between gender groups.

Employment Impact

Correlation found between employment status and reported depression levels.

Education Factor

Higher education levels showed a complex relationship with mental health outcomes.

Work & Study Hours Impact
