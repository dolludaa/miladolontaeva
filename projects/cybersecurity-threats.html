Cybersecurity Threats

Performed multi-dimensional analysis of global cyberattack data to detect long-term threat trends, financial exposure, and vulnerability-attack pairings.

Python Pandas Matplotlib

Project Overview

Collected, cleaned, and structured a large cybersecurity dataset, handled missing values, normalized categories, and prepared data for analysis. Used Pandas and NumPy for grouping, aggregation, and trend calculations across attack types, industries, and countries. Built custom visualizations in Matplotlib and Seaborn (time series plots, heatmaps, dual-axis charts, bar distributions) to explore attack dynamics and financial impact.

Role

Data Analyst

Skills

Python (pandas, NumPy, matplotlib, seaborn)
