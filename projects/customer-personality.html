Customer Personality Analysis

Retail customer data project focused on segmentation, spending behavior, and channel performance.

Excel Tableau Segmentation

Project Overview

Designed and delivered 5 interactive Tableau dashboards covering demographics, product spending, family type segmentation, and campaign response. Translated raw dataset into clear visual tools that enable stakeholders to understand customer groups and buying patterns at a glance.

Role

Business Analyst

Skills

Excel, Tableau, Segmentation, Data Cleaning, Dashboard Design

Key Segments

High-Value Customers

Small but profitable segment with high frequency and monetary value.

At-Risk Customers

Customers who haven't purchased recently but have high potential.

Discount Seekers

Segment highly responsive to promotions and price reductions.

Dashboard Visualizations

Segment Overview

This visualization provides a high-level view of the customer base, categorized into distinct segments based on purchasing behavior and demographics. It highlights the relative size and value of each group, allowing for targeted marketing strategies.

Understanding product preferences is crucial for cross-selling and up-selling. The following chart breaks down spending habits across different product categories for each identified segment.

Spending by Product

A detailed breakdown of spending habits reveals which product categories drive revenue for each customer segment. This insight helps in optimizing inventory and promotional offers.

Analyzing how customers interact with the brand is key to optimizing engagement. This next visualization examines the preferred channels—Web, Store, or Catalog—for each segment.

Channel Usage

This chart analyzes the preferred purchasing channels (Web, Store, Catalog) and the recency of interactions, helping to tailor communication strategies to the most effective mediums.

To drive growth, we need to focus on our most valuable customers. The deep dive below explores the characteristics of the high-value segment in greater detail.

High-Value Segment Deep Dive

An in-depth look at the high-value customer segment, identifying common traits and behaviors that distinguish them from other groups.

Demographics play a significant role in customer behavior. The final visualizations examine the impact of family structure and geography on purchasing patterns.

Family Type Analysis

Analysis of how family structure influences spending and product preferences.

Geographic Distribution

Mapping customer distribution to identify key geographic markets.
