Restaurant Tips Analysis

Analyzing tipping trends based on time, party size, and bill amount to understand diner behavior.

Tableau Excel Data Visualization

Project Overview

Analyzed a dataset of 244 restaurant transactions to uncover patterns in tipping behavior. The goal was to identify which factors—such as day of the week, time of day, and party size—most significantly influence tip percentages. Data cleaning was performed in Excel to ensure accuracy before visualizing the insights in Tableau.

Role

Business Analyst

Skills

Tableau, Excel, Data Cleaning, Dashboard Design

Key Findings

16% Average Tip

The overall average tip across all transactions hovers around 16%.

Friday Lunch Peak

Friday lunch service delivers the strongest tipping performance compared to other times.

Small Groups Pay More

Smaller groups, particularly solo diners, tend to leave higher tip percentages.

Dashboard Visualization

Restaurant Tips Analysis Dashboard

A comprehensive view of tipping metrics, including tip % by party size, day/time analysis, and the correlation between total bill and tip amount.
